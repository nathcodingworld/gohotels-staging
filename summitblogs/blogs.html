Dining at Summit Galleria Cebu

Booking a three-day weekend stay at Cebu? Indulge in a little luxury dining at Summit Galleria Cebu in Cebu City. The Café Summit will offer hearty meals inspired by Cebu’s rich gastronomic history. You can start the day with the restaurant’s hearty breakfast set, combine entrées with pasta and salads, and then end your course with delectable desserts.

To save time, you may also want to reserve a room at a hotel in Cebu for easy access to the city’s best restaurants and other top tourist destinations. The Summit Galleria Cebu Hotel combines comfort and style through its complete amenities and artistic contemporary design. Both your eyes and mouth will feast when you stay in this hotel.
