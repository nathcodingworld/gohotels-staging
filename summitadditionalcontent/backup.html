• About Hotel

    • Summit Ridge Tagaytay Hotel, which is part of the Robinsons Hotels and Resorts family of brands, is your next destination for the ideal romantic getaway, business trip, or special occasion in the city of Tagaytay.
    • Rooms and Amenities

      The hotel boasts a total of 108 rooms, letting guests choose between a Summit Suite, Deluxe King, or Deluxe Twin room. Our 18 suites come with dual bedrooms, their own living room, and a dedicated kitchen area. All rooms feature a stunning view of Taal Volcano and include essential creature comforts like beds with high-quality linens and hypoallergenic pillows, a flat-screen TV, a hot and cold shower, a coffee and tea nook, a mini chiller, an in-room safe, multiple power outlets, and a dedicated Wi-Fi router, among others.

      Summit Ridge’s recreational facilities are open to keep guests fit and relaxed during their stay. There’s a tranquil spa, a gorgeous infinity pool, an in-house gym, and sports courts like an indoor basketball court and badminton court. Hungry diners can enjoy savory treats from Cafe Summit, as well as at the numerous dining establishments found next door at Robinsons Tagaytay.

    • MICE and Other Major Occasions

      Appreciated for housing some of the largest banqueting and state-of-the-art Meetings, Incentives, Conventions, and Events (MICE) facilities in Tagaytay City, Summit Ridge Tagaytay stands out for its friendly, professional, and personable brand of service to all guests who are checked in for business. In addition, Summit Ridge Tagaytay’s banquets and events team is fully prepared to provide efficient and attentive service to ensure the success of every occasion held inside the hotel.

      We are ready to roll out dependable catering services, delicious custom-made menus, and custom-tailored venue settings—all of which will be executed flawlessly with a stress-free approach. Whether it’s for business, a family staycation, or a honeymoon, your stay at Summit Ridge Tagaytay will be one for the books.
