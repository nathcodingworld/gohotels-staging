Landmarks and Tourist Attractions Near Go Hotels Plus Naga City

Make Go Hotels Plus Naga City your home base before you go explore more of what the city has to offer. Here are some attractions that are worth hitting up when you’re in Naga:

  • The Basilica of Our Lady of Peñafrancia

    The Minor Basilica of Our Lady of Peñafrancia, the Patroness and Queen of Bicol, holds the distinction of being one of the largest Marian pilgrimage sites in all of Asia. Visit the home of the miraculous image of the Virgin Mary and, if you’re so inclined, say a prayer for your special intentions. ...

    St. John the Evangelist Metropolitan Cathedral

    Another religious site that you should visit when in Naga City is St. John the Evangelist Metropolitan Cathedral, or the Naga Metropolitan Cathedral. Admire the fine details in the church’s Romanesque architecture and glass windows, and snap a photo at the magnificent Portia Mariae arch that’s just a stone’s throw away. ...

  • Caramoan Islands

    The Caramoan Islands are known collectively as the Emerging Paradise of the Pacific due to their breathtaking white and pink sandy beaches, as well as their wealth of limestone cliffs and caves. Go on an island hopping adventure with loved ones just a few hours away from Naga City, and discover the unspoiled shores that were also the backdrop of the reality TV show Survivor....

    Mt. Isarog National Park

    To see more of Bicol’s natural wonders, head over to Mt. Isarog National Park, which is home to one of Bicol Region’s active volcanoes. Here you’ll encounter beautiful sights like Malabsay Falls at the foot of Mt. Isarog and Magragobrog Waterfalls inside the mountain’s crater. ...

  • Naga City Ecology Park

    Yet another green space you should explore is the Naga City Ecology Park, where you can picnic and stroll around with friends or family. The highlight of the park is its giant maze. If you’re up to the challenge, get lost in this labyrinth and see who among you in your group can find their way out first! ...

    Pinacuason Hot Spring Resort and Adventure Park

    Lastly, for both leisure and deep relaxation, consider making a stop at Pinacuason Hot Spring Resort and Adventure Park. Try out the park’s zip line or its bike zip line, which will allow you to pedal a modified bike along a cable that’s a hundred feet high. When you’re done, immerse yourself in the healing waters of one of the hot spring pools....
